About Iron Thread Co.

Founded in a garage and grown through hustle, Iron Thread Co. started with a single press and a mission: to create apparel that people actually want to wear. We believe great gear brings people together — from events to brands, crews to causes.

Today, we work with clients nationwide to design and produce custom clothing that's bold, durable, and always on-brand. Whether you're building a brand or hyping an event, we're here to make sure your message doesn't just land — it leaves a mark.
